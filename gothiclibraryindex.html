Calliope's Gothic Library

  • Horace Walpole's Castle of Otranto (1764)
    • The first gothic novel, a short, wild ride with giant helmets, surprise family members, one of gothic fiction's only nice priests, and Manfred, the gothic villain prototype.
  • Clara Reeve's The Old English Baron (1777/1778)
    • The second gothic novel, historically dedrided as a knockoff of Otranto. It inverts the usual narrative of supernatural disruption, tries harder to do historical fiction, and commits to the manuscript bit a little harder.
